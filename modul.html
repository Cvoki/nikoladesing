Modul speaker is designed on a platform that is built of five speakers inside, which are all
oriented in different directions and that causes the surround sound effect.

Project started with a careful research on how people consume music in some space, how can
they move around and hear the same sound- or how can more people in one space share that
experiance. That research provaded a solid base which led to creation of the ball shaped
concept inspired by the Moon.

Body of the speaker was made with a help of a rapid prototyping technologies, after which it
was refined with a putty and finaly painted in selected colors. All inside parts were purchased,
connected and carefully integrated into structural speaker boxes, so the quality of sound could stay on high level.
